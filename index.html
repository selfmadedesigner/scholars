About the Project

The High Impact Scholars Digital Repository (HISDR) is a digital archive that preserves and amplifies the knowledge and experiences of at-risk and exiled Latin American and Caribbean scholars, artists, and journalists.

What are Cafecitos?

Having a cafecito among friends is about sharing ideas as a community. Join us for an in-person or virtual cafecito where you can meet our scholars, hear their stories, and learn about their impactful work.
