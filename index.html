About the Project

The Mellon High Impact Scholars program is dedicated to supporting the scholarship and creative work of at-risk scholars from around the globe. Through mentorship, research opportunities, and community engagement, we aim to empower these scholars to make meaningful contributions to their fields and communities.

What are Cafecitos?

Having a cafecito among friends is about sharing ideas as a community. Join us for an in-person or virtual cafecito where you can meet our scholars, hear their stories, and learn about their impactful work.
