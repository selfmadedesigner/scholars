HIGH-IMPACT SCHOLARS INITIATIVE

Giving a voice to impactful artists, journalists, and scholars who matter

Empowering Voices, Inspiring Change

The Mellon High Impact Scholars initiative is dedicated to supporting scholars whose work and voices are at risk. Through mentorship, research opportunities, and community engagement, we foster an environment where creativity and scholarship can thrive, regardless of borders or barriers.

Our Partners

Mellon Foundation

The Mellon Foundation is a key partner in this initiative, providing essential funding and support to empower scholars facing adversity. Their commitment to the arts and humanities is instrumental in making this work possible.

College of Fine Arts, UT-Austin

The College of Fine Arts at The University of Texas at Austin is a proud institutional partner, offering a vibrant academic home and creative community for our scholars.

Caribe Digital Collective, UPR-Río Piedras

The Caribe Digital Collective at the University of Puerto Rico, Río Piedras, brings together artists, researchers, and technologists to support innovative scholarship and cross-cultural collaboration.
