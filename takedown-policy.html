Take Down Policy

High Impact Scholars Digital Repository (HISDR) is committed to ensuring that we have the appropriate rights to all the content that is included in this digital repository, be it as a freestanding material or as part of a presentation, resource or audiovisual piece. This commitment extends to protecting any privacy, legal or ethical rights of the people or organizations that may be alluded to in this repository.

How to Request Content Removal

If you are a copyright holder, a legal representative of a copyright holder or if you have been affected by any content of this repository, and you wish to contest any material, please contact the High Impact Scholars Digital Repository at info@highimpactscholars.org or by mail at Building, Street, Number, Town, State, Zip Code.

Please include in your communication the following information:

  • Full name
  • Contact information (email, phone, mailing address)
  • Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing any copyright, as well as privacy, legal or ethical rights.
  • Provide sufficient information to locate the material (URL, title, author, date of publication)
  • Reason for requesting the removal of the content (copyright infringement, privacy violation, unethical use, etc.)
  • Evidence to support your removal claim (proof of ownership, legal documentation, etc.)
  • A physical or electronic signed declaration of the copyright holder, affirming that the provided information is accurate and that you own the rights to the content
  • If you are an agent, also include an authorization to act on behalf of the copyright holder.

Process

HISDR will respond to any email correspondence within 5 business days. Physical mail correspondence will be replied to within 10 business days. In said time frame, if we are unable to determine that we are permitted to use the content in question, HISDR will cease to provide access to the material until and if it can conclude that the use of the material is legitimate. We may consult external legal resources before making our decision. If the request for removal is deemed valid, the content will be removed temporarily or permanently, depending on the case. If you have any questions or need more information, we will contact you before taking any action.
